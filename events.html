Discover Exciting Events in Los Angeles

Los Angeles is a city that never sleeps, brimming with vibrant events and activities year-round. From star-studded film festivals and cultural parades to live music concerts and food fairs, there's something for everyone to enjoy. Below, explore some of the top events happening across the city.

Hollywood Bowl Concert Series



Experience live music at the iconic Hollywood Bowl, featuring performances from world-renowned artists and orchestras.

Dates: Various dates throughout the year. Location: Hollywood Bowl, Los Angeles.

LA Food Fest



A must-visit for food lovers! This festival showcases diverse culinary delights from local chefs and food trucks.

Dates: March 15-17, 2025. Location: Grand Park, Downtown LA.

Griffith Park Outdoor Movie Nights



Enjoy classic movies under the stars with family and friends. Bring a blanket, snacks, and soak in the cinematic magic.

Dates: Summer months. Location: Griffith Park, Los Angeles.

Los Angeles Pride Parade



Join the celebration of diversity and inclusion at one of the largest pride parades in the world.

Dates: June 8, 2025. Location: West Hollywood.

Los Angeles Auto Show



Explore the latest in automotive design and innovation at this premier auto event.

Dates: November 22-December 1, 2025. Location: Los Angeles Convention Center.
